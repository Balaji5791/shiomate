How Miles Worth Works

Our platform makes it easy for drivers with empty trucks to find shipments and for companies to save on transportation costs.

For Drivers

  1. Register and create your driver profile with vehicle details
  2. Browse available shipments matching your route and vehicle
  3. Accept shipments and earn extra income
  4. Deliver goods and get paid securely

Earn 50% extra on empty return trips

For Companies

  1. Register and create your company profile
  2. Post your shipment details (pickup to delivery)
  3. Get matched with drivers on the same route
  4. Track shipment in real-time

Save up to 50% on transportation costs

Benefits for Everyone

  • Reduce environmental impact by eliminating empty miles
  • Increase logistics efficiency
  • Transparent pricing
  • Secure, vetted driver & company community
  • Real-time tracking
  • Rating system for quality service
